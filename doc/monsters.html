Enhanced Monsters
This component brings all monsters closer to their PnP counterparts. For this purpose, I used many books, from 2nd to 3.5 edition.
For each monster, I checked:
- name
- general / race / class
- ability scores
- gender
- morale
- alignment
- thac0
- level
- hit points
- animation
- armor class
- attack per round
- saving throws
- resistances
- experience
- hide in shadow / move silently
- scripts (all replaced by better ones)
- items (weapons and special)
- effects (special resistances and immunities)
- movement speed
- proficiencies (if applied)
- spells and innates

Optional :
- high constitution affect monsters hit points. Currently, most monsters don't have a constitution score and even if they had one, it would make no difference as their class don't give constitution bonus hit points. This way, a 8hd hobgoblin would have the same hp as a 8hd mountain bear. For each monster, I added these bonus hit points in the following form : [+x]
- experience. I think that PnP experience is inconsistent and inappropriate in some cases. I tried to make a new system based on hit dice and difficulty level.
